Vimanika Aeroflare: Forest Fire Monitoring UAV

Collaborators: Manvik Kumar, Advait Mishra, Rishav Mukherjee, Manaal Azhar, Devashish Bindal

Duration: May 2023 - April 2024

Proposal: Drive Link

Summary:
Vimanika Aeroflare is a next-gen UAV system designed for rapid and reliable wildfire detection across large terrains. Equipped with Through-Wall Machine Learning Sensors and a hybrid BVLOS navigation system, the drone offers real-time thermal and vegetation analysis for fire edge detection. With a 75-minute flight time, compact form factor (1.4m length, 1.8m wingspan, 19.9kg weight), and fan-in-wing design inspired by the Peregrine Falcon, Aeroflare is both agile and efficient. Its embedded intelligence enables autonomous decision-making, low-latency data processing, and seamless C2 operations via a custom-developed chatbot. Designed with FAA compliance, cost-efficiency, and investor-friendly communication strategies in mind, Aeroflare is a game-changing tool for sustainable forest management and disaster response.

EcoWave AgroHub: No-Waste Desalinator for Coastal Agriculture

Collaborators: Manvik Kumar, Aryan Daga, Rohan Gupta, Akshita Mangal

DurationJanuary 2023 - March 2024

Proposal: Drive Link

Summary:
EcoWave AgroHub revolutionizes coastal agriculture by solving water scarcity through an integrated, zero-waste desalination and energy recovery system. Using vacuum-based pumps to extract seawater and fish excreta, the system filters and processes this mixture through forward osmosis and multi-effect distillation for clean water output.

The innovation lies in its circular model—waste brine powers energy turbines to self-sustain the unit, and nutrient-rich fish excreta becomes organic fertilizer. Designed for efficiency, minimal environmental impact, and affordability, this multi-benefit solution empowers rural coastal communities with access to water, energy, and sustainable food production.

PlanetX: Multi-Source In-Situ 5-Way Oxygen Production

Collaborators: Manvik Kumar, Saksham Jain, Mannat Kaur, Mana Sharma

Duration: August 2023 - September 2023

Proposal: Drive Link

Summary:
PlanetX is a cost-effective, self-sustaining oxygen production and atmospheric synthesis system developed for off-Earth colonization. Inspired by ISRO’s low-cost tech philosophy, it integrates a network of chemical reactions optimized to repurpose waste and minimize resource depletion.

With deployments on Mars, Luna, and Europa, PlanetX adapts to diverse planetary environments, utilizing multiple in-situ sources for oxygen generation. The system embodies circular design principles—consumption stays below natural regeneration thresholds, ensuring long-term habitability.
